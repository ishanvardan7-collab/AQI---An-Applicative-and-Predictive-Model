**Project Overview**

**The Air Quality Index (AQI) Monitoring Website is a web-based application designed to provide users with an interactive and user-friendly platform to understand air quality conditions. The primary objective of this project is to spread awareness about air pollution and its impact on human health by presenting AQI data in a simple, clear, and visually appealing format.**

**Air pollution is a growing concern, especially in countries like India where many cities experience high pollution levels. AQI serves as a key indicator to determine how clean or polluted the air is and what potential health effects may arise. This project enables users to view AQI values along with their classifications such as Good, Moderate, Unhealthy, and Hazardous.**
